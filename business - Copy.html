Our Business Model

Harmony Bear and Barrel operates on a direct-to-consumer and boutique retail model, ensuring quality control and customer satisfaction.

Value Proposition

  • Purity: No additives or preservatives in our honey products.
  • Craftsmanship: Barrels are hand-made by skilled carpenters.
  • Sustainability: We reinvest 10% of profits into bee conservation.
  • Transparency: Full traceability from hive/home to customer.

Revenue Streams

Direct Sales

Online store sales via our website with shipping nationwide.

Wholesale

Supplying local gourmet shops and specialty grocery stores.

Workshops

Paid training sessions on beekeeping and barrel maintenance.

Key Partners

  • Local Beekeeping Associations
  • Sustainable Timber Suppliers
  • Eco-friendly Packaging Companies
  • Regional Logistics Providers
